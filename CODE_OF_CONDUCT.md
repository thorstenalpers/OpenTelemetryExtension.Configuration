# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus on the problem, not the person.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Publishing someone's private information without consent.
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by [opening an issue](https://github.com/thorstenalpers/OpenTelemetryExtension.Configuration/issues). All reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately.

## Enforcement

The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate this code of conduct.
